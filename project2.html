Agra
A UNESCO World Heritage Site and a symbol of love, along with impressive Mughal architecture like the Agra Fort and Fatehpur Sikri.
Rajasthan
Rajasthan, known for its majestic forts, palaces, and vibrant culture, showcases rich history and stunning desert landscapes.
Bhitargao
Bhitargaon, located in Uttar Pradesh, is renowned for its ancient brick temple dedicated to Lord Vishnu, dating back to the 5th century AD, making it one of the earliest examples of Hindu temple architecture in North India.
Maharashtra
Maharashtra, a vibrant Indian state, is known for its diverse culture, bustling cities like Mumbai, and scenic landscapes including the Western Ghats.
Rameshwaram
Rameshwaram Temple, located on Rameswaram Island in Tamil Nadu, is a significant Hindu pilgrimage site dedicated to Lord Shiva.
Nagaland
Nagaland, located in northeast India, is known for its rich tribal culture, vibrant festivals, and stunning landscapes.
East India
The "Seven Sisters" refers to the seven northeastern states of India: Arunachal Pradesh, Assam, Manipur, Meghalaya, Mizoram, Nagaland, and Tripura. Known for their diverse cultures, lush landscapes, and rich biodiversity.
Gujrat
Gujarat, located on the western coast of India, is known for its rich history, vibrant culture, and significant contributions to Indian economy and politics.
Darjeeling
Darjeeling, known as the "Queen of the Hills," is famous for its stunning tea plantations, breathtaking views of the Himalayas, and the historic Darjeeling Himalayan Railway.
Goa
Goa, renowned for its beautiful beaches, vibrant nightlife, and rich Portuguese heritage, offers a perfect blend of relaxation, adventure, and cultural experiences.
Varanasi
Varanasi, one of the oldest cities in the world, is a spiritual hub known for its sacred ghats along the Ganges River, where mesmerizing rituals and ancient traditions come to life.
Himalayas
The Himalayas, towering majestically across northern India, offer breathtaking landscapes, thrilling trekking opportunities, and a rich tapestry of diverse cultures and spiritual traditions.
Mysore
Mysore, famed for its regal heritage, is home to the stunning Mysore Palace, vibrant markets, and rich cultural traditions, making it a captivating destination for history and architecture enthusiasts.
